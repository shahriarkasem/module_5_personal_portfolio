Know our
Global Community

A global community is a rule-bound society of individual people who come together to share a particular set of concerns. People often identify with their culture and nation, but now they can also identify as participants in the "global community." This new identity is important because it opens up more avenues for constructive dialogue and facilitates possibilities for cooperation amongst those who would otherwise be competing rivals. Establishing common goals is one of the most effective ways to do this. Another tactic you might try is surrounding yourself with people who think similarly, or ones you know will push your thinking forward. One really cool way to do this: find a Quores user whose stance you disagree with and follow them instead!
